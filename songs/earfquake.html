EARFQUAKE

View Lyrics on Genius
Narrative Overview

EARFQUAKE presents Igor's initial feelings and the overwhelming impact of falling in love. It introduces the love interest from Igor's perspective and the instability this love is already causing, including over-dependence and abandonment issues. By establishing the complicated nature of the relationship from the outset, EARFQUAKE starts the story in media res, hinting at the cyclical aspect of this love story once again.

Emotions Expressed

EARFQUAKE expresses the complex emotions involved with forming a new love interest: infatuation, vulnerability, desperation, and fear.

Lyrical Breakdown
  • "For real, for real this time": Implies this isn't the first time Igor has felt these feelings before, but claims this time is different. Once again shows that this story has been repeated many times.
  • "'Cause you make my earth quake / Oh, you make my earth quake / Riding around, your love is shakin' me up / And it's making my heart break": Directly expresses the destabilizing and painful intensity of his feelings.
  • "Don't leave, it's my fault": Shows insecurity and a willingness to take the blame to prevent abandonment, hinting at previous mistakes Igor has made.
  • "I just need some confirmation on how you feel": Highlights the uncertainty and lack of closure that plagues Igor.
Musical Breakdown

Samples
