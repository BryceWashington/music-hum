I DON'T LOVE YOU ANYMORE

View Lyrics on Genius
Narrative Overview

In I DON'T LOVE YOU ANYMORE, Igor is actively trying to convince himself that he no longer loves his previous love interest. However, there's an underlying struggle and a hint that he's not entirely free. While he is no longer actively trying to be in a relationship, he can't lose his love for the person overnight, and fails to understand that. He has the right intentions, as shown in GONE, GONE / THANK YOU, but not the right method—this will be his downfall.

Emotions Expressed

I DON'T LOVE YOU ANYMORE expresses lingering attachment, frustration, and self-doubt. Igor knows he shouldn't love his love interest anymore, but can't stop himself.

Lyrical Breakdown
  • "I don't love you anymore": Repeated insistently, as if trying to make it true through sheer force of will. He lists reasons why they're incompatible and why he needs to move on—trying to justify the detachment.
  • "You wasted my time and I know that these things are not hard": Igor believes this shouldn't be a difficult process, showing his desire to force emotional detachment instead of letting it happen naturally.
  • "Now I'm stuck": An admission that he still can't fully move on. This sets up the final track, ARE WE STILL FRIENDS?, where he reaches for friendship and potentially restarts the emotional cycle.
Musical Breakdown
  • The music has a slightly strained and muffled quality, representing Igor's internal conflict—he wants to say he's no longer in love, but it's not entirely true.
  • The pitched-up, almost "angry toddler" vocal delivery in the chorus underscores his emotional immaturity and lack of peace with the situation.
  • Solange's background vocals add an ethereal contrast, offering a sense of clarity and beauty beneath Igor's messy emotional state.
Samples

I DON'T LOVE YOU ANYMORE samples Caramel by Cluster, which provides the melodic foundation of the track. Tyler muffles the sample, symbolizing how Igor is being dishonest with himself—masking clarity with distortion.
