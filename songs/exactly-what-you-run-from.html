EXACTLY WHAT YOU RUN FROM YOU END UP CHASING

View Lyrics on Genius
Narrative Overview

EXACTLY WHAT YOU RUN FROM YOU END UP CHASING is a thematic interlude, narrated by Jerrod Carmichael, that foreshadows the cyclical and self-sabotaging nature of Igor's pursuit.

Emotions Expressed

The interlude creates a sense of inevitability and foreshadows struggle.

Lyrical Breakdown
  • "Exactly what you run from, you end up chasing...Giving it everything that you can. There's always an obstacle.": This line encapsulates the central conflict of the first half of the album. Igor is in relentless pursuit despite fear and obstacles.
Musical Breakdown
  • A brief, spoken-word track over a minimal beat.
  • Carmichael's calm, conversational delivery provides a moment of clarity and direct thematic statement amidst the emotional swirl of the music.
